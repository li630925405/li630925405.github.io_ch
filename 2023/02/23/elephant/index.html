Rubber Elephant

Posted on 2023-02-23

Translated from <Time Bear, Mirror Tiger, and Invisible Kitten> by Fan Ye

范晔 - 《时间熊，镜子虎和看不见的小猫》 - 《橡皮象》

Once upon a time, there was no man on Rubber Planet. Not at all.

God first created man with stones. The stone man was bulky. Their moods were heavy too. Later they became tortoises in the desert.

God then created man with wood. The wood man was stiff. Their heads were stubborn too. Later they became monkeys in the forest.

God finally decided to create man with rubber. The rubber man is light, soft, and flexible.

The rubber man has another merit: after they do regrettable things, shameful things, things they want to forget, they can erase them using their own rubber. The rubber man is happy.

But rubber will be used up. When the rubber is finished, the rubber man disappears.

God is sad. He decided to become an enormous rubber elephant.

For every regret, shame, and unforgettable painful memory in the rubber man’s whole life, the rubber elephant will erase them using his own body.

从前，橡皮星球上没有人。一个也没有。
神先用石头造人。造出来的人非常笨重。心情也很沉重。后来变成了荒漠中的陆龟。
神又用木头造人。造出来的人非常僵硬。头脑也很固执。后来变成了丛林里的吼猴。
神最后决定用橡皮造人，造出来的人轻盈柔软又有弹性。
橡皮人还有一个优点：做了后悔的事，羞愧的事，想要忘记的事，都可以用自己的橡皮擦掉。橡皮人很快乐。
但是橡皮会越用越小，到最后用完了，橡皮人就消失了。
造橡皮人的神很难过。他决定把自己变成一头巨大无比的橡皮象。
每个橡皮人一生中最大的后悔，羞愧，痛苦得想要忘记的事，橡皮象都用自己的身体帮他们擦掉。
